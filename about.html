About Us

Meet HouneTeam

A Japan-based indie studio with a passion for mobile gaming.

Our Story

HouneTeam is an independent game studio based in Japan, dedicated to creating engaging mobile gaming experiences. We started with a simple belief: games should be fun, accessible, and rewarding for every player.

Our first title, Lumo Idle Park, combines relaxing idle mechanics with satisfying tycoon progression. It represents our approach to game design — build something players genuinely enjoy coming back to.

We're a small team with big ambitions. Every game we make is crafted with care, tested with real players, and improved through data-driven iteration.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we build.

Mobile First

We design exclusively for mobile, optimizing every interaction for touch-based experiences and on-the-go play sessions.

Player Driven

Every decision starts with the player experience. We listen to feedback, study behavior data, and iterate to make games people truly enjoy.

Quality Craft

Made in Japan with attention to detail. We believe in polishing every aspect of a game before putting it in players' hands.
