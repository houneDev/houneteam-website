Lumo Idle Park

LiveAndroid

An idle park that keeps running when you close the app. Trees produce resources on timers. Boxes fill and move to the warehouse automatically. Upgrades extend capacity, unlock new zones, and increase output.

  • Offline income: resources keep accumulating when the app is closed
  • Speed and Rare boosts
  • Multiple zones, each with its own production chain
  • Cloud save via Google Play Games
